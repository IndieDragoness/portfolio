BACK SUMMARY

Engineering Disc Drives.

Employed in R&D at a Fortune 500 Disc Drive Manufacturer.

Test Process Lead Engineer

At this company, I served as a Test Process Lead Engineer developing a Python-based test framework for overseas factories for two years. This work challenged me to integrate myself into a massive code-base (millions of lines of code), become familiar enough with leadership goals to give direction to other engineers, and deploy tests to live manufacturing sites for production. Development required me to maintain good communication with remote engineers overseas, and I was responsible for tens of thousands of testbed drives used in the development of new technologies.

An example of one of the efforts I pioneered involved finding Disc Drives that tested at what was considered exceptional standards, called 'Gold Standard' drives in our workplace. Previously, engineers were manually comparing batches of test drives by reading logs; I automated the comparison process and built a robust Python-based system to compare many drives to 'Gold Standard' drives, highlighting variations and discrepancies. This saved the company many engineering man hours.

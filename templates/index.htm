About Me

Hi! I'm Sage and this is my portfolio, delivered via Python Flask web-app running on Microsoft Azure's Cloud Services.

I'm a Senior Software Engineer and Military Veteran that started in the software engineering field in 2013, and loves tech! The reason I love tech is because I've seen it change lives and lead to quality employment for so many, including myself; I tend to view it as a means to really good ends. That being said it's also been a fun journey of writing python scripts to automate disc drive testing and perform machine learning, doing game development and volunteer particle accelerator research in my spare time, and contributing my experience during my military service. As the only engineer in my family, I've relied on wonderful mentors at various Employers to help me get this far, and I'm grateful to be here.

I'm open to Senior Software Engineering positions, especially ones that utilize Python, Linux, and Microsoft Azure.
